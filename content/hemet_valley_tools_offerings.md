# Hemet Valley Tools: Services, Products, and Rentals Catalog

This document establishes the official product lines, rental catalog structure, and service offerings for **Hemet Valley Tools** (Hemet Valley Tool & Supply).

---

## 1. Positioning: "The B2B and Contractor Partner"

While similar to national industrial rental chains like **United Rentals** (which dominates heavy earthmoving, scissor lifts, and industrial site scaffolding), **Hemet Valley Tools** occupies a highly specialized, hyper-local niche:

*   **Agility & Pro-Grade Focus**: We bridge the gap between heavy civil machinery rentals and fine-finish contracting supply.
*   **The Cordless Powerhouses**: We are a premier regional supplier and authorized service dealer for **Makita** and **Milwaukee** tool ecosystems, serving both heavy construction contractors and local tradespeople.
*   **Legacy Knowledge**: We combine rentals with an elite, on-site, 41+ year repair facility, which national rental chains typically do not offer to the public.

We carry Milwaukee's top-tier professional contractor lines:

We carry Makita's legendary dust-extraction, fine-woodworking, and heavy construction equipment:

*   **AVT® (Anti-Vibration Technology)**: Heavy-duty demolition hammers designed to minimize operator fatigue on commercial concrete jobs.
